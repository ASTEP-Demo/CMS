Opening the CATO GDPR Search Utility

To open the CATO GDPR Search utility, perform the following steps:

1.   Click Start from the task bar > All Programs > Causeway > Causeway GDPR Search.

The Causeway Licence Manager login page is displayed.

2.   Enter the organisation administrator's Email address and Password, as provided by Causeway.

3.   Click SIGN IN.

The GDPR Search window is displayed.
